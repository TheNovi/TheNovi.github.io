Programming

I wrote my first 'Hello world!' program at the high school. It was a C# window application. Later that year I tried making my first game for LudumDare.
The second year of school we were learning Java. And that year my friend told me about Python. Next year I started my Music Player (see Projects).
After finishing high school, I managed to get at CVUT FIT. But only for a very short time, because I started to hate C and didn't understand higher mathematics. So I quit.
Got a job in a near Hotel, and started learning programming myself.

Gaming

Everything started when I got a PlayStation 2 for my 5-7th birthday (don't remember exactly). There I got hooked on a game named 'Jak and Daxter' which was funny because I didn't know any English.
After some time (I think it was a gift for my 10th birthday) I got a PlayStation 3, great times.
But the most important moment was my 15th birthday when I got my first notebook and new age started. Also, that was the time I started going to high school where my programming lives started.
