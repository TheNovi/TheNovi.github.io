Who am I?


I am just 20 years old programmer and gamer from Czech Republic.
If you want to know more (for some reason). contact me.

Programming

I wrote my first "Hello world!" program at the high school. It was in C# window application.
That was the time when I got an idea for a small assistant, but Google was quicker. Also that year I tried making my first game for LudumDare.
The second year of school we were learning Java. And that year my friend told me about Python. Next year I started my Music Player (see Projects).
After finishing high school, I managed to get at CVUT FIT. But only for a very short time, because I started to hate C, don't understand integrals. So I quit.
Get a job in a near Hotel, and started learning programming myself.

Gaming

Everything started when I got PlayStation 2 on my 5-7th birthday (don't remember exactly). There I got hooked on my favorite game "Jak and Daxter" which was funny because I didn't know any English (it didn't change much since then ).
After some time (I think it was on my 10th birthday) I got PlayStation 3, ahh great times.
But the most important moment was my 15th birthday when I got my first notebook and new age started. Also, that was the time I started going to high school where my programming lives started.
Since then I have played hundreds of games, just switching them, never really got hooked to any of them.
When I was 20, I got my new awesome desktop computer and here we are.
Right now I am mostly playing osu!.
One thing is for sure if the game is top played/streamed game, I don't play it. It's not like I hate it because it's popular or anything. I just don't enjoy (idk why). I always more liked 2nd/3rd tier games.
